Office

Our office is ENS 19N.

Directions:

  1. When you enter ENS from the main entrance, go left into the stairwell.
  2. Underneath the stairs going up is the door to the stairs that lead to the basement.
  3. Once you exit the stairwell, turn left and follow the hallway all the way down to the computer lab (ENS 31NR).
  4. Head to the smaller room at the back of the lab.
  5. The double doors on the right are our office!
